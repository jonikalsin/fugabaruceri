Gia Carangi Biography

Gia Carangi is best known as Fashion/Runway Model who has an estimated Net Worth of $10 Thousand. Fashion model of the 1970s and '80s who many considered to be the first supermodel. She was born on January 29,1960, Philadelphia, PA. She appeared on the cover of the April 1979 fashion magazine Vogue. After moving to New York, she became a favorite of fashion photographers Francesco Scavullo, Arthur Elgort, and Joseph Petrellis. Angelina Jolie portrayed her in HBO's 1998 biopic film Gia. Her father, Joseph, was a restaurant owner, and her mother, Kathleen Carangi, was a homemaker. She was raised with her two older brothers. Her zodiac sign is Aquarius
