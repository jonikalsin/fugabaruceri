Biography

Curtis Berry was born in Selma, AL on June 16, 1959. Power forward known for his time with the Kansas City Kings and the Lancaster Lightning. After he retired from professional basketball, he became a coach at Mount Vernon Presbyterian School in Atlanta. During his time with the Kansas City Kings, he played alongside Cliff Robinson. Prior to his work as a professional basketball player, he attended the University of Missouri where he averaged 6.8 points in 1978. Upon graduation, he became a third round pick in the 1981 NBA Draft.
