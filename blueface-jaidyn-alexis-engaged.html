Blueface Gets Engaged To Jaidyn Alexis: 'I Got A Real B-tch'

Blueface has gotten engaged to Jaidyn Alexis and wasted little time in using the news as as ammo against his mother, with whom he’s been at odds for some time.

The California rapper popped the question to his girlfriend during the Los Angeles Rams vs. Pittsburgh Steelers game at SoFi Stadium on Sunday (October 22).

A video of the proposal captured Blueface getting down on one knee and popping the question. After she said yes, he kissed an emotional Jaidyn Alexis and placed a ring on her finger.
