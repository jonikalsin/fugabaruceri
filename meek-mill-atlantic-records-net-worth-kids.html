What Are Meek Mill's Accusations Against Atlantic Record? His Net Worth Insights, Kids, &amp; Girlfriend

American Rapper Meek Miller has accused Atlantic Records of trying to separate him from Roddy Rich and Rick Ross on a series of tweets posted on 2 February 2022. He tweeted how the Atlantic record couldn't drop music until 9 months after his last album got blackballed. He shared how his record label made over $100 million on him and they cut him out of managing Roddy Rich when he was behind bars.

Roddy my youngin still …. But that label seperated us instantly when the millions came in from him… same thing they tried to do with me and rozay … it takes a long time to catch up too because most of em tied in for the love of some money …artist scared to speak up

— Meek Mill (@MeekMill) February 1, 2022

He wrote,

"Roddy my youngin still ... But that label separated us instantly when the millions came in from him...same thing they tried to do with me and rozay...it takes a long time to catch up too because most of em tied in for the love of some money .. artist scared to speak up."

Meek also tweeted that Atlantic Records didn't pour any cash into his Expensive Pain project and they were not allowed to drop his album for nine months which is the time for the ending of his deal with the label. He also said that he wasn't scared to speak against them because he isn't dependent unlike other rappers of the company.

Meek encouraged other artists to speak up against the manipulative labels and tell the truth saying, "Other artists speak up and tell the truth while you winning not when you are down!" He then claimed how they bought Roddy to him and cut him as soon as the money came and said that he didn't get a dollar from the label. He tweeted,

"Also I had to fight for my life to get dc4 counted as an album because they said I 'called it a mixtape' if ya'all made 20 million off it what is it counted as? "

His last tweet explained that he and Roddy are good now and wrote,

"they would of let us go to war and made the money still .. I had to say I'm rich that's my youngin and turn to these people and ask wassup for the work they asked me to do! I ain't get a dollar"

While Meek ranted all his allegations on Twitter, his label, Atlantic Records has offered no comment as to their official response.

Net Worth Of Meek Mill

Meek Mill has an estimated net worth of $20 million. He is considered one of the popular rappers from United States. He started his career in rap music by participating in rap battles as a teenager. He was in a rap group, The Blackhoundz with his three friends.

He released the mixtape, Flamerz in 2007, and its follow-up, Flamerz 2- Hottest in the City and this got him signed to Rick Ross' Warner Brothers Music Label, Maybach Music Group. He released his debut studio album, Dreams, and Nightmares in 2012 and it reached number one in the U.S. Rap and HipHop R&B charts as well as on the Billboard 200 chart. His other albums were also hit such as Dreams Worth More Than Money, Wins and Losses, Young and Gettin it, and more.

Some of his popular tracks are Make 'em Say, Rose Red, Mr. California, Ima Boss, Tupac Back, Dreamchasers, and its volumes. Meek Mill also has his own label, Dream Chasers Records named after his mixtape series and it was launched in October 2012. He has worked with many popular artists like Nicki Minaj, Drake, Justin Timberlake, Tory Lanez, Post Malone, Chris Brown, Ed Sheeran, and many more. 

Meek Mill also owns many luxury cars and properties and he has endorsed many brands like Monster Energy. 

Meek Mill's Girlfriends Details

Meek Mill has been linked with many popular faces. He has dated Fahimah Raheem from 2006 to 2012. They even had a son together named Papi in 2011.

He then dated model Lira Galore in 2013. Meek was also in a fling with K. Michelle in 2014. The same year he was also reportedly dating Model Jai Nice.

Meek Mill and Nicki Minaj's relationship gathered much attention as they were considered a power rap couple. But their relationship only lasted for two years ending in 2017. The rapper Mill dated model Bernice Burgos in 2018.

Meek Mill was spotted hanging out with fashion designer Milan Harris in 2019 but they never confirmed their relationship. Harris even denied the rumors of dating him saying that he was her first celebrity client and that he's her friend.

However, the couple shares a child together and Mill had tweeted about it writing, "Milano dropped me off a king on my birthday!" He also confirmed the pregnancy news while he was in a feud with Nicki Minaj sharing that his girl being pregnant and watching his tweet about his ex was very clownish.

But the rapper announced in 2020 that they decided to remain friends and build their child as separate parents. He wrote, "we still have mad love for each other but we both came to an understanding."

The rapper Meek Mill is a father of three children and his other two sons are from his past relationship; they are named Murad and Rihmeek.
