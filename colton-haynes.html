Colton Haynes Hollywood Life

Bio:
Colton Haynes (born Colton Lee Haynes in Wichita, Kansas on July 13, 1988) is an American actor and model. He began modeling at age 15 and his first success came with an Abercrombie & Fitch campaign. He also modeled for J.C. Penney, Kira Plastinina, and Ralph Lauren. He is best known for his role as Jackson Whittemore on MTV’s Teen Wolf. He also played Brett Crezski on ABC’s The Gates. He has had guest appearances on several television shows, including The Hills, CSI: Miami, The Nine Lives of Chloe King, and Look.

Best Known For:
Colton Haynes is best known for his role as Jackson Whittemore on MTV’s Teen Wolf, and as Roy Harper on CW’s Arrow.

Personal Life:
Colton married celebrity florist, Jeff Leatham, on Friday, October 28, after getting engaged on March 12. Colton came out as gay in May 2016.
