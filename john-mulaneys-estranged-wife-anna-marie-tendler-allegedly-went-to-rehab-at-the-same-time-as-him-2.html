John Mulaneys Estranged Wife Anna Marie Tendler Allegedly Went to Rehab at the Same Time as Him

John Mulaney‘s split from wife Anna Marie Tendler was revealed last week and now more details have emerged about what happened between them.

As many people probably know already, John went to rehab at the end of last year and completed a 60-day stay for cocaine and alcohol addiction.

Now, a new report is claiming that Anna Marie was in rehab at around the same time as him.

Click inside for more details…

Page Six reports that Anna Marie “went into therapy at Silver Hill in Connecticut” after her husband also went into rehab. The news was first reported on the Fame, Ain’t It A Bitch podcast by columnist AJ Benza.

“Anna Marie was in rehab at Silver Hill for [emotional]… and eating disorders,” the source told Page Six.

The source also says she didn’t talk about issues with her husband while in rehab. They said, “She didn’t talk at all about that, we were all on our own journey of recovery. But it is clear she suffered during their marriage.”

Anna Marie is reportedly “doing better” and focusing on her art right now. Read the statement she released when they both announced their split.
