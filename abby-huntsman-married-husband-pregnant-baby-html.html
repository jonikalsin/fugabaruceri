Abby joined The Cycle in July 2013 filling a vacancy by the departure of S.E. Cupp and later as the second replacement host after Ari Melber.

Abby Hunstman's Bio: Age & Career

Born on 1 May 1986, Abigail Haight Huntsman was raised in Utah. She graduated with a degree in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics from the University of Pennsylvania. The 32-year-old journalist started her career with a position behind-the-scenes at Good Morning America.

Abby interned for Diane Sawyer while attending the University of Pennsylvania and in 2012, she appeared as a political commentator for ABC News in Washington, D.C., and Good Morning America in New York City. 

It wasn't long before Abby was profiled in ABC News' Five Questions, a section of This Week with George Stephanopoulos and was interviewed by Brian Willams. Abby joined Fox News Channel in October 2015 as a general assignment reporter. 

Abby Huntsman Joins Women Day Time Talk

It was officially announced in August 2018 that Abby Huntsman would be joining The View as a co-host for its upcoming 22nd season.

Abby will be joining moderator Joy Behar and Whoopi Goldberg, Sunny Hostin, and Meghan McCain. Abby showed her excitement on Instagram about getting to join one of the most successful talk show on television adding that it was a dream come true.

Senior executive producer of the show, Hilary Estey McLoughlin was equally excited to welcome Abby to the show, mentioning about her smartness and distinctive point of view on everything from politics, to pop culture to parenthood.

Abby & Her Husband's Life Perfected By Baby Girl's Arrival

Abby Huntsman, a general assignment reporter for the Fox News Channel, is leading a beautiful married life with her life partner, Jeffrey Bruce Livingston and her adorable daughter, Isabel Grace.

Abby married her college sweetheart Jeffrey in the year 2010 in a wedding ceremony held at the Washington National Cathedral. Both the partners were of 24 years of age at the time of the wedding.

The affection and the bond between the couple become stronger after the birth of their first daughter. 

During the Fox & Friends Weekend show on 17th June 2017, Abby invited her husband, and there she revealed that she was pregnant with a baby girl. On 17th November 2017, Abby even flaunted her big baby bump in an Instagram shot where she expressed that she couldn't wait to meet her baby girl.

Finally, after seven years of marriage, Abby and Jeffrey welcomed their bundle of joy, Isabel Grace in November 2017. 

She shared an adorable picture of her daughter on 29th November 2017 saying that her little angel had arrived and they had no idea that their heart could be this full.

Since the birth of the little Isabel, Abby's Instagram account has been flooding with cute updates of her daughter. It looks like the couple is enjoying every single moment of their life with their daughter and loving her to the fullest.
