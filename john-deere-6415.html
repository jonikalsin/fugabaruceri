John Deere 6415 Horsepower, Price, Specs, Oil Capacity, Reviews

John Deere 6415 Utility Tractor is equipped with a John Deere 4045 turbocharged diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled 276 ci [4.5 L] engine, which delivers 100 hp [74.6 kW] power at rated rpm 2300.

John Deere 6415 Utility Tractor featured a John Deere 4.5L 4-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD/4×4 MFWD 4WD, hydrostatic power steering, 5028 lbs [2280 kg] Rear lift (at 24″/610mm), hydraulic wet disc brakes, and 7695 to 8987 pounds weight and 16.8 qts [15.9 L] engine oil capacity.

John Deere, 6415 Utility tractor attachments include John Deere 620, and John Deere 640 front-end loader.
