King Von Had A Net Worth Of $750k When He Died

Born Dayvon Daquan Bennett on August 9, 1994, King Von grew up in Chicago, a city known for its vibrant rap scene. As a teenager, he became involved in street life and faced numerous legal troubles, including charges related to violence and drugs. However, his passion for music provided an outlet for his experiences and emotions.

King Von gained prominence in the music industry through his affiliation with fellow Chicago rapper Lil Durk. He released his debut mixtape, Grandson Vol. 1, in 2019, which garnered attention for its raw and gritty storytelling. His lyrics painted a vivid picture of the realities of street life, resonating with audiences seeking an authentic representation of the Chicago drill rap genre.

King Von Was Killed In October 2020

Tragically, King Von’s life was cut short on November 6, 2020, at the age of 26. The circumstances surrounding his death sent shockwaves through the music industry and his dedicated fan base.

Investigations into his death reveal that on that fateful day, the late rapper got into a violent altercation with Quando Rondo in a parking lot outside the Monoco Hookah Lounge in Atlanta, Georgia.

Von was shot in the process and was rushed to the hospital in critical condition. He did not survive the surgery that was supposed to save his life, and was pronounced dead later that day. The primary cause of his death was the gunshot wounds he sustained from the fight.

Further reports from the Georgia Bureau of Investigation, indicated that seven others were affected in the fight, with two of the seven also joining Von in death. The primary suspect in the investigation was Timothy Leeks, who was also coincidentally one of the victims. King Von was buried On 14th November 2020, in Chicago, Illinois.

Despite his untimely demise, King Von’s impact on the rap scene remains undeniable. His authentic storytelling and raw talent continue to inspire aspiring artists, cementing his legacy as one of the notable voices of Chicago drill rap.
