Candace Rondeaux is an award-winning American investigative journalist. She serves as a columnist on World Politics Review, a website that publishes news related to international politics.

Besides, she serves as the Senior Director for the Future Frontlines program at New America, an open-source public intelligence service for next-generation security and democratic resilience. She also directs the Planetary Politics initiative, a cross-disciplinary program that aims to find solutions to the wicked problems posed by digitization and decarbonization in a multipolar world.
