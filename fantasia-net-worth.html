Fantasia Net Worth - NewsNow Nigeria

Fantasia Monique Barrino, born on 30th June 1984, in High Point, North Carolina, is a singer who gained fame after winning the third season of the television show “American Idol” in 2004. Her debut album “Free Yourself” attained Double Platinum certification, selling over two million copies and receiving four Grammy nominations. Fantasia’s estimated net worth is $3 million as of the end of 2017. She has been active in the entertainment industry since 2004.

Fantasia was brought up in High Point by her parents, Joseph and Diane Barrino, both singers. Despite a difficult childhood, she immersed herself in music. In 2004, after attending the Atlanta auditions of “American Idol”, she won the competition with over one million votes ahead of the runner-up, Diana DeGarmo.

Subsequently, Fantasia signed a contract with J Records, releasing her first album “Free Yourself”, which debuted in the top ten of the American charts, selling over two million copies. Her second album, “Fantasia” and subsequent albums gained her three more Grammy nominations.

Aside from her music career, Fantasia has also ventured into Broadway, winning the World Theatre Award for her role in the Broadway production “The Color Purple”. With a successful career, she has accumulated a net worth of $3 million.

In 2015, she married businessman Kendall Taylor, with whom she has two children. Fantasia’s personal struggles, triumphs, and achievements have fascinated many, making her a relatable figure for her audience.
