Chrisean Rock: I Wish I Wasnt Pregnant

Chrisean Rock has decided that the pregnant life isn’t for her, proclaiming that she wishes she wasn’t expecting a child with longtime boyfriend Blueface.

Taking to Instagram Live on Saturday (April 22), the reality TV personality shared that she was no longer excited about the prospect of being a mother.

“I wish I wasn’t pregnant,” she said. “I was excited at first, but now, it’s like — it’s exhausting. Honestly, I don’t know what I’m doing. I’m probably not in the mood for none of the baby traditions and shit.”

The IG Live was captured by The Neighborhood Talk, which you can check out below.

Chrisean Rock’s pregnancy journey has certainly been up and down so far. Earlier this month, she took to Twitter to share a heartfelt message she’d penned to her unborn child, where she said in part that the child is going to help her be the best version of herself.

“Hey sweet angel I can’t wait till u get here,” Rock wrote. “You gon help me be the best me. I’m looking forward to you bud [pregnant emoji] … the fact it’s precious to know you right here with me is beautiful.”

But back in March, Blueface’s mother Karlissa Saffold claimed that the mother of her soon-to-be grandchild was actually faking her pregnancy for clout.

Saffold made the comments on her Instagram Story after her son’s girlfriend was seen flaunting her stomach on Blueface’s Instagram Live. Saffold claimed Rock was purposefully sticking out her stomach, and that there was no way she could be merely two months pregnant.
