Kubota B2100 Price, Specs, Review, Attachments 2022

Kubota B2100 Price, Specs, Reviews, Horsepower, Weight, Engine Oil Capacity, Attachments, History & Pictures

Kubota B2100 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with Kubota D1005-D10 diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled 61.1 ci [1.0 L] engine, which produces 21 hp [15.7 kW] power at rated rpm 2600.

Kubota B2100 Compact Utility Tractor featured with Kubota 1.0L 3-cyl diesel engine, 6 forward and 2 reverse gears, 3.6 qts [3.4 L] oil capacity, 4×4 MFWD 4WD, power steering, wet disc brakes, and 1280 lbs [580 kg] Rear lift (at ends).

Kubota B2100 Compact Utility Tractor attachments include a mowing deck, snowblower, front-end loader, and backhoe.
