Where will Leagues Cup 2024 be held?

The Leagues Cup, a prestigious tournament featuring teams from the Major League Soccer (MLS) and Liga MX, is set to captivate football fans across North America from July 26 to August 25, 2024.

With 47 first-division clubs from Canada, Mexico, and the United States participating, this World Cup-style tournament promises to be an unprecedented event in the realm of North American socc er. 

Tournament Format and Structure

The 2024 Leagues Cup will follow a format similar to its inaugural 2023 season, with the participating 47 teams contesting a group stage before a 32-team knockout bracket determines the eventual winner.

The competition will feature a total of 77 matches, with all 29 MLS clubs and 18 Liga MX clubs participating in this prestigious tournament.

The tournament's point system includes regulation wins counting as 3 points, and each team will receive 1 point if the game is tied after 90 minutes, with the winner of the subsequent penalty shootout earning an additional point.

Group Stage and Ranking

To ensure geographical and competitive balance, the participating clubs, except the two Leagues' champions, have been divided into three tiers based on the Leagues Cup Ranking, forming 15 groups of three clubs each.

The top two teams from each group will advance to the Round of 32, with the tournament prioritizing fair and balanced competition among the 45 clubs.

Hub Privileges for Liga MX Clubs

One of the notable features of the 2024 Leagues Cup is the introduction of the "hub" system, which aims to reduce the travel burden for deserving Liga MX clubs.

The four highest-ranking Liga MX clubs are granted "hub" privileges that enable them to avoid excessive travel by playing in pre-determined locations as the home team.

The hub privileges are based on the Leagues Cup Ranking, with each club assigned a "host city" that allows them to avoid traveling up to a certain point in the tournament .

Hub Privileges for Top LIGA MX Teams

The reigning LIGA MX champion, Club América, and the three top-ranked LIGA MX clubs have been granted hub privileges, which minimize travel and reward the Mexican clubs by playing in pre-determined locations.

Each of these top-ranked clubs has been assigned a specific host city and will enjoy the benefits of hosting matches in familiar territories, thereby reducing their travel fatigue and providing them with a competitive edge.

Leagues Cup 2024 Venues

The Leagues Cup 2024 will be held across Canada and the United States, featuring matches hosted at MLS stadiums throughout the two countries.

The tournament's structure ensures that all matches will be available for streaming on MLS Season Pass on the Apple TV app in over 100 countries and regions, with select matches also being available on various television networks such as TelevisaUnivision, FOX Sports (FS1), TSN, and RDS. This extensive coverage ensures that fans worldwide can witness the thrilling action of the tournament.

Hubs for Top LIGA MX Teams

The top LIGA MX teams granted hub privileges will be playing in pre-determined locations, thereby minimizing travel and providing them with a competitive advantage.

Club América, CF Monterrey, Chivas Guadalajara, and Tigres UANL have been assigned specific host cities and venues for their matches, ensuring that they can compete in familiar environments without the added stress of extensive travel.

Historical Significance and Qualification Rewards

The Leagues Cup 2024 holds significant historical importance as it will determine three qualifiers for the 2025 Concacaf Champions Cup, offering a pathway for clubs to potentially represent the region at the FIFA Club World Cup.

The tournament not only showcases the best of North American football but also provides a platform for clubs to earn prestigious spots in the continental club championship and the revitalized Copa Interamericana, further enhancing the tournament's significance in the world of football.

CONCACAF Champions Cup Qualification

Similar to the previous year, the 2024 Leagues Cup winner will earn a berth in next year's continental club championship, the CONCACAF Champions Cup.

The winner of the Leagues Cup will receive a bye in the First Round, earning passage straight through to the Round of 16, underscoring the high stakes and rewards associated with this tournament.

 

On the whole, the Leagues Cup 2024 promises to be an exhilarating event that brings together the best of MLS and Liga MX in a fierce and captivating competition.

As the tournament unfolds across various venues in Canada and the United States, football fans can expect an action-packed month filled with thrilling matches and intense competition, ultimately leading to the crowning of the Leagues Cup champion for 2024.

The Leagues Cup 2024 will not only showcase the prowess of North American football but also provide a pathway for clubs to compete on the international stage, making it a significant and eagerly anticipated event in the world of soccer.
