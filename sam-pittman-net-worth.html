Sam Pittman’s Career and Net Worth

Sam Pittman is an American football coach who has a net worth of $5 million and an annual salary of $3 million as the head football coach at the University of Arkansas. Born in El Reno, Oklahoma in November 1961, Pittman played defense end for Pittsburgh State before starting his coaching career.

He coached high school football from 1986 to 1990 and then moved on to Hutchinson Community College from 1991 to 1993. Pittman worked as an offensive line coach at various universities including Northern Illinois, Cincinnati, Oklahoma, Missouri, and Kansas. He also served as an assistant head coach at Western Michigan, Northern Illinois, and North Carolina.

Pittman finally became the head coach at Arkansas in 2020 after working as an assistant head coach and offensive line coach at Arkansas from 2013 to 2015 and the offensive line coach at Georgia from 2016 to 2018. He has become known for his coaching skills and has amassed a net worth of $5 million throughout his career.
